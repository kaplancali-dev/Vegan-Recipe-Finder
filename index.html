🌿 Vegan Recipe Finder

Enter what you have — find vegan recipes you can make right now

Check off what's in your kitchen and instantly see what you can cook — all recipes are 100% vegan and gluten-free. Filter out allergies like peanuts, tree nuts, soy, coconut, corn, mushrooms, or nightshades with one tap. Smart substitutions are built in: plant-based milk (any) covers almond, oat, soy, cashew, and more. Any cooking oil covers olive, coconut, avocado, and others.

⭐ Next to each ingredient, tap the star to save it to your Staples — ingredients always in your home that never need re-selecting. Tap Load My Staples anytime to pre-fill them instantly.

🔍 Browse & Search

  1. Filter by category, cook time, allergies, or recipe name. Try typing protein bar to see all matching recipes instantly.
  2. Load your Staples or tick off what you have on hand.
  3. Each card shows your % match, missing items, and nutrition figures per serving.
  4. Tap 🔍 Find Recipe to open it, or 🛒 Add Missing to build your shopping list.
  5. Save with ❤️, add notes with 📝, or copy the full recipe to paste into your Notes app.

🧑‍🍳 What Can I Make?

  1. Choose your mood — Meal, Dessert, Sauces, or All.
  2. Load your Staples or check off what you have.
  3. Recipes group by readiness: 🟢 Make Now  ·  🟡 Missing 1  ·  🔴 Missing 2–3
  4. Tap 🛒 Add Missing to List, then print or copy before heading to the store.
