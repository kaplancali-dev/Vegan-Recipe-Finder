Quick Start

HARVEST matches over 1,000 vegan recipes to what's already in your kitchen. Here's how to get started:

Step 1 — Stock your pantry

Head to the 🥬 My Pantry tab:

  1. 📌 Add Staples — tap the category buttons (Oils, Spices, Grains, Beans, etc.) to save things you always keep on hand. These stay saved forever.
  2. Add Ingredients — type in perishables like fresh produce, herbs, and fruits, separated by commas. Perishables are highlighted in orange so you use them first.

Step 2 — Find recipes

  1. 🔍 Browse All — every recipe, ranked by match. Tap Filters for cuisines, cook time, and allergens.
  2. 🍳 Ready to Make — recipes you can make now with 1 or fewer missing ingredients.
  3. ❤️ Favorites — your saved recipes. Tap 🤍 on any card to add.
  4. 🛒 Shopping — organized by recipe with automatic GF substitutions.

Features

  1. 📌 Want to Make — tap on any card to add it to your Shopping list with missing ingredients.
  2. 🍳 I Made This — log your cooks. History is tracked in Favorites.
  3. 🤍 Collections — file favorites into collections (Weekly Meal Plan, Weeknight, Game Day, etc.).
  4. ⚠️ Allergens — set in the Filters drawer to hide recipes with specific ingredients.
  5. 🛒 GF Swaps — shopping list auto-swaps soy sauce → tamari, flour → GF flour, etc.
  6. 🔄 Substitution Hints — green hints appear when a good ingredient swap exists.
  7. ☁️ Cloud Sync — back up across devices via My Pantry.

Privacy Policy

HARVEST does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your ingredient lists, staples, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is stored securely in your own Supabase account and is never shared with third parties. We use basic analytics to understand how the app is used — no tracking, no ads, ever.
