📖 How HARVEST Works ▾

You open your fridge and pantry. You see ingredients. But the real question is always the same: "What can I actually make right now?"

HARVEST was built to answer that — instantly.

Created by an experienced food creator, this app turns what you already have into real, nourishing meals you'll actually crave. No more scrolling endlessly. No more wasted ingredients. No more guesswork.

Instead, HARVEST connects your kitchen to a curated world of top, nutrition-focused plant-based chefs — bringing you over 1,000 recipes designed to be simple, flexible, and deeply satisfying.

The goal isn't just to cook more — it's to make cooking feel effortless again. ❤️

Your Kitchen, Unlocked

Clean, Flexible, Intentional

100% plant-based, sourced from trusted chefs, filterable by allergen, cuisine, mood, or time. Some recipes use soy sauce or flour tortillas — ingredients that contain gluten. Rather than exclude those recipes, we fix them: soy sauce → tamari, flour tortillas → corn tortillas. One simple swap keeps the recipe — and makes it fully gluten-free.
