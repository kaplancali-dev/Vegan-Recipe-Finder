Quick Start

1. Stock your pantry

  1. Go to 🥬 My Pantry and tap 📌 Add Staples — choose Oils, Spices, Grains, Beans, and anything else you always have on hand.
  2. Use the Add Ingredients input to type items separated by commas. These go into your active ingredient list for matching. Staples are added separately via the 📌 Add Staples grid.

2. Find recipes

  1. 🔍 Browse All ranks every recipe by how well it matches your pantry. Use Filters for cuisines, cook time, and allergens.
  2. Sort by nutrition — tap Protein ↓, Fiber ↓, or Low Cal in the sort bar to find the healthiest version of whatever you're craving.
  3. 🍳 Your Matches shows only recipes you can cook right now (1 or fewer missing ingredients).

3. Plan, shop, cook

  1. Tap 📌 My Queue on any recipe card to save it. When you're ready to shop, tap 🛒 to send it to Shopping.
  2. 🛒 Shopping shows only recipes you've sent — with checkboxes for each missing ingredient. Tap 📤 to share the list.
  3. After cooking, tap ✅ I Made This to rate it 1–5 stars and log whether you'd make it again. You'll also get a quick option to share the recipe with a friend.

4. Track what you've made

  1. Made It (inside ❤️ Favorites) is your cooking journal — it shows every recipe you've cooked, the date you first made it, and your star rating.
  2. Tap the stars to update your rating anytime. Tap ✕ to remove an entry.
  3. My Queue's History sub-tab shows the same log with sortable columns — sort by recipe name, rating, date, or "Would make again."

Good to know

  1. ❤️ Favorites — organize saved recipes into collections like Weekly Meal Plan, Celebration, or Game Day.
  2. Substitution hints — when you're missing an ingredient and a good swap exists, a green hint appears (GF and lower-sugar alternatives too).
  3. Ingredient benefits — tap any ingredient in a recipe to see evidence-based health info.
  4. 📤 Share — share recipes or shopping lists via text, email, Notes, or social media. After rating a recipe you just cooked, a "Share with a friend" button appears for 5 seconds.
  5. ⚠️ Allergens — set in My Pantry or in the Filters drawer on Browse and Your Matches to hide recipes with specific allergens.
  6. ☁️ Cloud Sync — open the ☰ Menu and tap Cloud Sync. Enter your email, verify with a one-time code, then push your data to the cloud or pull it down to any device. No password needed.
  7. 🌟 Recipe of the Day — a new high-protein recipe spotlighted daily on the Browse tab, matched against your pantry.

Phone toolbar

When installed on your phone, a toolbar appears at the bottom with four buttons:

  1. 📤 Share — share a recipe or the app itself via text, email, or social media so friends can add HARVEST to their home screen too.
  2. 🔄 Refresh — reload the app to get the latest recipes and updates.
  3. ☰ Menu — open the menu for Instructions, About, Feedback, and Cloud Sync.
  4. 🌙 Night Mode — toggle dark mode for easier reading at night.

About Us

HARVEST was created by an experienced team of food creators who wanted a better way to answer the daily question: "What can I make right now to help ensure I'm thriving?"

Our database of over 4,500 recipes brings together the best of the world's top plant-based chefs and creators — from Pick Up Limes and Woon Heng to The Korean Vegan and Hot for Food. Whether you're craving Korean comfort food, Italian pasta night, or a quick Indian curry, HARVEST introduces you to creators and cuisines you might never have discovered on your own.

Every recipe is 100% plant-based and nutrition-focused. The goal isn't just to cook more — it's to expand your palate, try something new, and make cooking feel effortless again.

Privacy Policy

HARVEST does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your ingredient lists, staples, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is stored securely in your own Supabase account and is never shared with third parties. We use basic analytics to understand how the app is used — no tracking, no ads, ever.
