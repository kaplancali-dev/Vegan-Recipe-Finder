You open your fridge and pantry. You see ingredients, but the real question is always the same:
"What can I actually make right now?" HARVEST was built to answer that, instantly.

HARVEST connects your kitchen to a curated world of top, nutrition-focused plant-based chefs—bringing you over 1,000 recipes designed to be simple, flexible, and deeply satisfying.

Your Kitchen, Unlocked — Tell HARVEST what's in your kitchen and instantly see what you can make — plus a smart shopping list for anything missing.
Clean, Flexible, Intentional — 100% plant-based, sourced from trusted chefs, filterable by allergen, cuisine, mood, or time. Some recipes use soy sauce or flour tortillas — ingredients that contain gluten. Rather than exclude those recipes, we fix them: soy sauce → tamari, flour tortillas → corn tortillas. One simple swap keeps the recipe — and makes it fully gluten-free.
