Quick Start

HARVEST matches 1,000+ vegan recipes to what's already in your kitchen. Here's how to get started:

Step 1 — Stock your pantry

Head to the 🥬 My Pantry tab. You'll see two sections for adding items:

  1. 📌 Add Staples — tap the category buttons (Oils, Spices, Grains, Beans, etc.) to save things you always keep on hand. These stay saved forever, so you only need to do this once.
  2. Add Ingredients — scroll down and type in perishables like fresh produce, herbs, and fruits, separated by commas. Perishables are highlighted in orange so you remember to use them before they go bad.

Step 2 — Find recipes

Now check out the other tabs:

  1. 🔍 Browse All — every recipe, ranked by how well it matches your pantry. Use the search bar to find recipes by name or ingredient (try "chickpeas" or "pasta"). Tap Categories to filter by cuisine (Mexican, Thai, Italian…) or adjust the max cook time slider.
  2. 🍳 Ready to Make — only shows recipes you can make right now with 1 or fewer missing ingredients. This is the fastest way to decide what to cook tonight.
  3. ❤️ Favorites — all the recipes you've saved. Tap the 🤍 button on any recipe card to add it here.
  4. 🛒 Shopping — your shopping list, organized by recipe. Each recipe you've marked "Want to Make" appears as its own card with its missing ingredients. Tap 📝 to send a single recipe's list to Notes, or "All to Notes" for everything. You can also favorite or log "I Made This" right from here.

Other handy features

  1. 📌 Want to Make — see a recipe you'd like to try later? Tap "Want to Make" on the card. It appears in your Shopping tab as its own card with the missing ingredients listed, so you can shop by recipe.
  2. 🍳 I Made This — tap this button on any recipe card or shopping card to log that you cooked it. Your cooking history is tracked in the Favorites tab so you can see what you've made and when.
  3. 🤍 Favorite + Collections — tap the heart to save a recipe. You'll be asked to file it into a collection (Weekly Meal Plan, Weeknight, Game Day, etc.). Tap 📚 All in Favorites to see everything in one place.
  4. 🔄 Substitution Hints — if a recipe is missing one ingredient but you have a good swap (e.g. tamari for soy sauce, oat milk for almond milk), a green hint appears on the card suggesting the substitute.
  5. 📤 Share — tap the Share button on any recipe to send it to a friend. They'll get a direct link to the original recipe — no app needed.
  6. Recipe details — tap any recipe card to see the full ingredient list (with checkmarks for what you have), nutrition info, and a link to the original instructions. You can also add personal notes.
  7. 🌙 Dark Mode — toggle dark mode on or off in the About section below.
  8. ☁️ Cloud Sync — optionally sign in to back up your pantry, favorites, and shopping list across devices.

💡 Add it to your home screen for the best experience — it works like a native app with offline support.

About Us

HARVEST was created by an experienced food creator who wanted a better way to answer the daily question: "What can I actually make right now?" Every recipe is 100% plant-based, sourced from trusted nutrition-focused chefs and creators. The goal isn't just to cook more — it's to make cooking feel effortless again.

Privacy Policy

HARVEST does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your ingredient lists, staples, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is stored securely in your own Supabase account and is never shared with third parties. We use basic analytics to understand how the app is used — no tracking, no ads, ever.
