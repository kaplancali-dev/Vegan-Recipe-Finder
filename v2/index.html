You open the fridge and think, "What can I make with this?" HARVEST knows. It matches your ingredients to plant-based recipes from the world's top nutrition-focused chefs and creators, prioritizes your perishables so nothing goes to waste, filters out your allergens, and builds a shopping list for the rest — whether you're cooking for yourself on a Tuesday or feeding a table full of friends, HARVEST makes the process both fun and easy.

Every recipe is gluten-free by default. When a recipe calls for something like soy sauce or flour tortillas, HARVEST highlights the swap right on the card — tamari, corn tortillas — so you never have to think about it.

Quick Start

  1. Add your ingredients — type what's in your fridge and pantry above, separated by commas. Fresh produce is automatically grouped as perishables.
  2. Set your staples — tap the category buttons in "Add Staples" to save items you always have on hand (oils, spices, grains, etc.).
  3. Browse recipes — switch to "Browse All" to see every recipe ranked by how well it matches your pantry. Use filters to narrow by cuisine, cook time, or allergens.
  4. Ready to cook? — the "Ready to Make Now" tab shows only recipes you can make with 1 or fewer missing ingredients.
  5. Build a shopping list — tap the cart icon on any recipe card to add its missing ingredients to your shopping list.

💡 For the best experience, use HARVEST on a larger screen (tablet or desktop) so you can see more recipes at once. Search works live across recipe names and ingredients.

About Us

HARVEST was created by an experienced food creator who wanted a better way to answer the daily question: "What can I actually make right now?" Every recipe is 100% plant-based, sourced from trusted nutrition-focused chefs and creators. The goal isn't just to cook more — it's to make cooking feel effortless again.

Privacy Policy

HARVEST does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your ingredient lists, staples, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you enable Cloud Sync, your data is stored securely in your own Supabase account and is never shared with third parties. We use basic analytics to understand how the app is used — no tracking, no ads, ever.
